How to preserve tomatoes without a fridge

Extend shelf life by up to 3 weeks and reduce food waste.

bolt Quick Summary

The secret lies in the "Stem-Down" method. By placing tomatoes stems down on a flat surface, you prevent moisture from escaping and bacteria from entering the fruit.

The Salt and Oil Method

This traditional Nigerian preservation technique is perfect for large batches of blended tomatoes when you don't have electricity.

  • • Boil your blended tomatoes until all the water evaporates.
  • • Add a generous amount of vegetable oil—it acts as a natural seal.
  • • Stir in a tablespoon of salt per liter of paste.
  • • Store in a clean glass jar in a cool, dark corner.

The Dry Ash Method

An ancient but effective technique used in rural areas. Fine, dry wood ash can preserve fresh tomatoes for up to a month by absorbing excess moisture and acting as a natural antiseptic.
